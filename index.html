The Art Of Andrew

Who am I? Well I am glad that you asked. I am a fictional character so I don't exist. Infact, I am not even an artist, I am no one. I do not know my purpose and I don't even know how I got the name "Andrew". I guess it is the name of the person that is creating this website, but I don't even know what websites are. Websites don't exist in a world that dosn't exist.

"Daring" - ArtTimes

One of the greatest artists of the 21st century, Andrew completely changed the course of art history with his revolutionary way of painting. He called it "The Brush BLOOM" a trending method in which artists apply a minimal amount of paint onto the canvas to create a figure. Truly an amazing idea from the master of art.
